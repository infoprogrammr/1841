You are well aware of ongoing elections in Uttar Pradesh. Mainly Three Major parties BJP,SP,BSP Party are the one which can form the government.You will be given count of votes in 10 important city of UP. Now the winning party from that city has certain rules:

1. If the number of votes are divisible by 2 , BJP wins from that city.

2. If the number of votes are divisible by 3 Samajwadi Party wins from that city

3.If number of votes are divisible by 7, BSP party wins from that city.

4.If number of votes are not divisible by any of three, another party wins the city.

5.If the number of votes is divisible by both number , then both party form coalition government in that city..

6.If the number of votes is divisible by all 3 number , then all party form coalition government in that city..

Then Who will win ? :

Party getting more number of city under them wins the election.

Note - There will be no case of clash.
